Carbonara recipe

Description

A classic carbonara recipe with pancetta, eggs, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Pancetta
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water
  2. Cut the pancetta into cubes
  3. Heat the pancetta in a pan
  4. Whisk the eggs with the Parmesan cheese
  5. Season with black pepper
  6. Drain the spaghetti
  7. Add the spaghetti to the pan with the pancetta
  8. Remove the pan from the heat
  9. Add the egg mixture to the pan
  10. Stir until the eggs are cooked
  11. Season with salt and pepper
