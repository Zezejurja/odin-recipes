Pancakes recipe

Description

A classic pancake recipe with maple syrup and butter.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Maple syrup

Steps

  1. Mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl
  2. Add the milk, eggs, and melted butter to the bowl
  3. Whisk until smooth
  4. Heat a pan
  5. Pour the batter into the pan
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface
  7. Flip the pancake
  8. Cook until golden brown
  9. Serve with maple syrup and butter
