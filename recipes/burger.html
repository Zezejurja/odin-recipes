Burger recipe

Description

A classic burger recipe with all the fixings.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Mayonnaise
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Tabasco sauce
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Burger buns

Steps

  1. Chop the onion and tomato
  2. Heat the butter in a pan
  3. Add the onion and tomato to the pan
  4. Season with salt and pepper
  5. Cook until the onion is soft
  6. Remove the onion and tomato from the pan
  7. Form the ground beef into patties
  8. Season the patties with salt, pepper, Worcestershire sauce, and Tabasco sauce
  9. Heat the pan
  10. Cook the patties in the pan
  11. Toast the burger buns
  12. Assemble the burger
